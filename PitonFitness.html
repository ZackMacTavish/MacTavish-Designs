PROJECT DETAILS
Piton Fitness

Piton Fitness is a fitness app for people of all levels. The owner reached out to me in hopes to take his website, and mobile app designs to the next level. The goal was to create a brand identity, website, and app designs for a future release in the app store. I chose to enhance the brand with a neomorphic style, photography, and a great user experience. The website and app are currently due for launch in 2020.
