PROJECT DETAILS
Vayner Sports

Vayner Sports is an agency for the modern day athlete. A subsidiary of Vayner X run by Gary Vaynerchuk. The focus was to show how sports players can invest and become active in ventures aside from their main profession. The goal was to create a bold refresh of their current website. Through current UI trends, neon colors, and high-contrast photography I came up with a design that is bolder than their prior site.
